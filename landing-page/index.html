About Us

Welcome to Abugida

Abugida is a platform which encompasses all courses taught online and is an alternative to a traditional classroom learning experience. It also compromises a discussion room where you can ask questions and provide answers for a particular question asked. Its advantageous for you as a student to exchange ideas and cooperate on various learning activities. This way, you will have an overall better learning experience. we build this website as a portfolio project for ALX software engineering program on may 2022.
